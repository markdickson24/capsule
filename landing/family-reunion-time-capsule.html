Family Reunion

A family reunion time capsule.

Everyone's angle of the same weekend. Cousins, aunts, uncles, and grandparents all drop their photos into one private album — then it opens for the whole family at once, whether that's this weekend or next year's reunion.

Why family reunions

One album, every generation.

A reunion is the rare time the whole family is actually in one place — but everyone's photos end up scattered across a dozen phones and never seen again. A family reunion capsule collects them all in one spot, so the group can relive the whole weekend together, from every angle.

  • – Every relative adds their own photos — no one has to be the designated photographer
  • – Relatives who couldn't make it can still contribute old photos ahead of time
  • – Unlock the night everyone's still together, or wait until next year's reunion
  • – Vote on family superlatives when it opens — Life of the party, Best candid, and more
How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

01

Start a capsule

Pick a name, set the unlock date, and decide who can add. Open this weekend, or next year's reunion.

02

Everyone adds in

Every relative drops in photos and videos from their own perspective — no one sees a thing until the day.

03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for the whole family at once — every angle, at one time.
