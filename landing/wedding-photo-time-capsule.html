Weddings

The wedding photo time capsule.

Every guest's photos, opened when the chaos settles. Instead of a camera roll nobody looks at again, your wedding photos come together in one place — sealed until the moment you choose to open them.

Why weddings

Guests capture what the couple can't see.

The couple is at the center of their own wedding — which means they miss almost everything happening around them. A wedding photo time capsule collects every guest's angle in one private album, then reveals it all at once.

  • – Guests drop in photos and videos from their phones — no group text, no forgotten shared album
  • – Nobody can preview the album, not even the couple, until the unlock date arrives
  • – Unlock on the one-year anniversary, the morning after, or any date you choose
  • – Guests can keep adding right up until a contribution deadline you set
How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

01

Start a capsule

Pick a name, set the unlock date, and decide who can add. Open by date, when everyone's together in person — or both.

02

Guests add in

Everyone drops in photos and videos from their own perspective — no one sees a thing until the day.

03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for every member at once — the whole day, every angle, at one time.
