Community Guidelines

Effective June 10, 2026

Capsule is a place to share memories with people you trust. These Community Guidelines apply to everything you create, upload, or share in the app once it's available. They exist to keep Capsule safe and welcoming. Breaking them can lead to content removal, loss of access to a capsule, or account suspension.

The basics

  • Be respectful of the people you share with.
  • Only share photos and videos you have the right to share.
  • Respect the privacy of anyone who appears in your content.

What's not allowed

  • Illegal content or activity of any kind.
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexual content involving minors. We have zero tolerance and will report it to the appropriate authorities.
  • Pornographic or sexually explicit content.
  • Harassment, bullying, threats, or hate speech targeting people based on race, ethnicity, religion, disability, gender, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics.
  • Graphic violence or gore, or content that promotes self-harm.
  • Sharing someone's private information or images of them without their consent.
  • Spam, scams, impersonation, or attempts to deceive other users.

Consent & privacy

Capsules are private and invite-only. Only add people who want to be part of a capsule, and think before sharing content that includes others — make sure they're okay with it.

Reporting & blocking

If you see content or behavior that breaks these guidelines, you can report it or block the user from within the app. Reports are reviewed, and we may remove content or take action on accounts as needed. Blocking is private — the blocked person isn't notified.

Enforcement

Depending on the severity, we may remove content, remove someone from a capsule, restrict features, or suspend or terminate accounts. Serious violations may be reported to law enforcement.

Age

Capsule is for people 13 years and older.
