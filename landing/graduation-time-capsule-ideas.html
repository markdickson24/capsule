Graduation

Graduation time capsule ideas.

Lock it now. Open it when you've made it. Senior year moves fast — the last dorm hangout, the final home game, the summer before everyone scatters. A graduation time capsule holds all of it until you're ready to look back.

Why graduation

A capsule for the whole chapter.

Not just the highlight reel — the whole friend group's version of senior year, freshman year, or the four years in between. Everyone contributes their own photos and videos, and nobody sees anything until the group decides it's time.

  • – Start it early — freshman year, a big trip, or the first day of senior year
  • – Every friend adds their own photos and videos right up to a deadline you set
  • – Unlock at graduation, at the first reunion, or on any date that feels right
  • – Vote on yearbook-style superlatives when it opens — Best photo, Most likely to…
How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

01

Start a capsule

Pick a name, set the unlock date, and decide who can add. Open by date, at graduation, or when you're all back together.

02

Everyone adds in

Every friend drops in photos and videos from their own perspective — no one sees a thing until the day.

03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for the whole group at once — every angle, at one time.
