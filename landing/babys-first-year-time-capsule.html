Baby's First Year

A baby's first year time capsule.

Every milestone, sealed until the candles. Grandparents, aunts, uncles, and close friends each drop in their own photos over the year — then everyone unlocks the whole story together on the first birthday.

Why baby's first year

Family near and far, all in one place.

Not everyone who loves this baby lives close by. A first-year time capsule lets family members contribute their own moments as they happen, no matter the distance — and turns the first birthday into a real reveal, not just another photo dump.

  • – Grandparents and family near and far each drop in their own moments
  • – Nothing is visible early — not even to the parents, if you turn on Surprise mode
  • – Unlock together on the first birthday, or any date that means something to your family
  • – A year's worth of milestones, revealed all at once instead of trickling in one at a time
How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

01

Start a capsule

Pick a name, set the unlock date, and decide who can add. Open on the first birthday, or any date you choose.

02

Family adds in

Everyone drops in photos and videos from their own perspective — no one sees a thing until the day.

03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for the whole family at once — a year of milestones, at one time.
