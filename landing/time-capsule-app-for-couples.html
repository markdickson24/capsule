Couples

A time capsule app for couples.

Not every couple lives in the same city — or even the same time zone. Capsule lets you both drop photos into one private album as you go, then unlock it together, on your terms: an anniversary, a visit, or the day you're finally in the same room.

Why couples

Built for the distance, not around it.

Long-distance couples and military families already know the ache of a missed moment. A shared, time-locked album turns waiting into anticipation instead — something you're both looking forward to, not just getting through.

  • – Both partners add photos privately, from wherever you each are
  • – Set an unlock date — a specific anniversary, or a date you'll finally be together
  • – Proximity unlock: the capsule can open the instant you're physically reunited, no date required
  • – Nothing to peek at early — the anticipation is part of the point
How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

01

Start a capsule

Pick a name, set the unlock date, and decide who can add. Open by date, when you're together in person — or both.

02

Both of you add in

Each of you drops in photos and videos as they happen. Neither of you sees a thing until the day.

03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for both of you at once. Every photo, at one time.
