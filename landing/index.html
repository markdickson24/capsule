How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

01

Start a capsule

Pick a name, set the unlock date, and decide who can add. Open by date, when everyone's together in person — or both.

02

Everyone adds in

Invite your people to a private album. They drop in photos and videos from their own perspective — no one sees a thing until the day.

03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for every member at once. Every angle, every perspective — the whole story at one time.

Inside the app

What it actually feels like to use.

Locked until the exact moment

Photos go in, but the album stays sealed. No early access, no sneak peeks — not even for the person who made it. A live countdown builds until it opens for the whole group at once.

The lock

Not even you can peek

Turn on Surprise mode and the creator is locked out alongside everyone else. You'll know how many memories are inside — but not one of them, until the date arrives.

Surprise mode

Open it only when you're all there

Turn on proximity unlock and the capsule won't open until every member is physically in the same place. The memories surface the instant you're reunited.

Together

Both sides of the story

A built-in dual-cam mode captures front and back lenses at once — the moment, and your reaction to it. Split-screen or picture-in-picture.

Dual camera

Yearbook-style superlatives

When a capsule unlocks, the group votes on categories — Best photo, Most likely to… Private, anonymous voting, winners revealed at the close.

Awards

Private by default, safe by design

Capsules are invite-only. Friend requests, reporting, and blocking are built in — the only memories you keep are the ones you want.

Safety

The wait is the whole point.

Anyone can share a photo the second it's taken. Capsule saves it for the moment you're all back together — and opens the whole story at once. That shared gasp? That's the feature.
