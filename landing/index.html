How it works

Three steps. One unforgettable reveal.

STEP 01

Create a capsule

Pick a title, add a cover, and set the unlock date. Choose to open by date, or only when everyone's together in person.

STEP 02

Invite your people

Add friends to a private, invite-only album. Everyone drops in photos and videos — but no one can see them yet.

STEP 03

Open it together

When the moment arrives, the capsule unlocks for every member simultaneously. The whole story, revealed at once.

🔒 The lock

Locked until the exact moment.

Photos go in, but the album stays sealed. There's no early access, no sneak peeks, no one person seeing it first. A live countdown ring builds the anticipation until it opens for the whole group at once.

  • ✓ Set any unlock date — a week away or a year out
  • ✓ Optional contribution lock so the lineup is final before reveal
  • ✓ Everyone unlocks at the same second, together

📍 Unlock together

Open it only when you're all there.

Turn on proximity unlock and the capsule won't open until every member is physically in the same place. No remote reveals — the memories come back the instant you're reunited.

  • ✓ Each member checks in with one tap
  • ✓ Unlocks when everyone's within range
  • ✓ Mix & match: by date, in person, or both
📸 Dual camera

Both sides of the story.

A built-in camera with a dual-cam mode that captures the front and back lenses at the same time — the moment and your reaction to it. Photo or video, split or picture-in-picture.

  • ✓ Front + back in a single shot
  • ✓ Split-screen or PiP layouts
  • ✓ Tap for a photo, hold for video

🏆 Awards

Yearbook-style superlatives.

When a capsule unlocks, the group votes on fun categories — Best photo, Most likely to… Suggest your own, upvote the best ones, and watch the winners revealed anonymously when voting closes.

  • ✓ Members suggest & upvote categories
  • ✓ Private, anonymous voting
  • ✓ Winners revealed at the close
🛡️ Just your people

Private by default. Safe by design.

Capsules are invite-only. Send and accept friend requests, keep your circle close, and rely on built-in reporting and blocking so the only memories you keep are the ones you want.

  • ✓ Friend requests & private invites
  • ✓ Report & block, baked in
  • ✓ You control who sees what
